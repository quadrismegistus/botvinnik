# Privacy Policy — botvinnik

_Last updated: 2026-07-20_

botvinnik is a chess trainer that runs entirely on your device. It is free
software (GPL-3.0-or-later); the source is at
<https://github.com/quadrismegistus/botvinnik>.

## We do not collect your data

botvinnik has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of
any kind. We do not have a server that receives your information, because
there is nothing to receive: everything the app does happens on your device.

- **Your games, settings, and practice progress** are stored only on your
  device (in the app's own storage). They never leave it, and we never see
  them.
- **We collect no personal information**, no usage statistics, and no
  identifiers. There is nothing to opt out of because nothing is gathered.

## Network use

**The mobile and desktop apps make no network requests at all.** Every chess
engine runs on-device — there is nothing to download and nothing to phone
home to.

The **web version** (botvinnik.app) has one optional exception: if you choose
one of the "Maia" bots, the app downloads that bot's neural-network model
(about 3.5 MB) once from HuggingFace, a public model host. This is a request
for a file — no personal data is sent, and the download is cached so it
happens only once. If you never pick a Maia, no third-party request is ever
made.

## Children

The app is suitable for all ages and, collecting no data, complies with
children's privacy rules by design.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new
date. Because the app collects nothing, changes would only ever concern network
behavior like the optional model download above.
